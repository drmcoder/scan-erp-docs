## When NOT to Choose Scan ERP

We're honest about fit — these scenarios point you elsewhere:

- **500+ operators, multi-factory** → Use WFX, PROTRACKER, or SAP
- **Brand managing vendors** (not a factory) → Use TrackIT
- **PLM integration is critical** → Use Centric, Aptean, or WFX
- **You need full PMTS (predetermined motion time)** → Use GSD instead
