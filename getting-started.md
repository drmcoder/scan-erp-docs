[← Back to home](/scan-erp-docs/)

## Is Scan ERP right for your factory?

Scan ERP is purpose-built for **CMT (Cut-Make-Trim) garment factories with 10-300 sewing machines**. Best fit:

✅ Factories in India, Bangladesh, Vietnam, Cambodia, Ethiopia, Nepal
✅ Operations with 100-500 bundles/day
✅ Piece-rate-based wage systems
✅ Limited or unreliable internet at the factory floor
✅ Budget under $20K/year for production tracking software

❌ Not the right fit if:
- You're a 1,000+ operator factory (need enterprise — see [WFX, PROTRACKER alternatives](https://scan-erp.web.app/blog/wfx-alternative-scan-erp-comparison.html))
- You're a brand managing vendor production (use TrackIT)
- You need PLM integration with Centric/Aptean

## Implementation Timeline

- **Days 1-14:** Master data entry (operations, SAM values, operators, machines, styles)
- **Days 15-30:** Pilot on one sewing line (10-15 operators)
- **Days 31-60:** Roll out factory-wide
- **Days 61-90:** Stabilize, tune SAM values, integrate with attendance/payroll

## What You Need to Provide

Hardware:
- 1× Raspberry Pi 4 (we ship pre-configured) — $80
- 1× TSC label printer (or your existing printer) — $200-500
- $50/operator Android phones (or BYOD)
- Existing biometric attendance device (ZKTeco supported)

Network:
- Factory WiFi (any speed works — system caches locally)
- One static IP for the print server (we configure)

People:
- 1 IT/admin person for 2-3 weeks during rollout
- Operators: 30-min training each
- Supervisors: 1-hour training
